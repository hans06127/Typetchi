# Typetchi v0.6.2

Version: 0.6.2

## Bugfix focus

- Close and collapse are separate widget states.
- Close hides Typetchi completely from the page and persists `closed: true` in `chrome.storage.local`.
- Collapse keeps the small handle visible and persists `collapsed: true`, `closed: false`.
- The extension action popup provides a 「顯示 Typetchi」 button that restores the widget without resetting pet progress, stats, position, or size.
- The collapsed handle can be dragged; drag movement is detected so releasing after a drag does not also expand the widget.
